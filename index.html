About Me

I'm an aspiring full stack developer currently enrolled in Rutgers' Coding Bootcamp. I graduated from Rutgers with a double major in IT and Psychology in 2015. I've had a passion for web design and development since being introduced to it in an Intro to Computer Science class in high school. I worked as a Web Coordinator at Porcelanosa, a luxury European kitchen, tile and bath firm, for three years. I was part of the marketing department and worked on national campaigns along with updating and maintaining the corporate website.During my time there I realized that instead of managing projects, I wanted to be a developer.

I want to have a career where I can work on different projects and tasks instead of having only one responsibility, and being a web developer can make this possible. I enjoy a challenge, which is something that developers encounter when there are bugs that need to be fixed, and I love the fact that this is an ever-changing field where there is always room to grow and learn something new.
